Creamy Treats

Hi! Welcome to Creamy Treats!

Creamy Treats is a small food business focused on homemade cakes and pastries. We create simple, comforting desserts made to be shared—perfect for everyday cravings and family celebrations.

Who is the company for?

Creamy Treats is for families and individuals who want affordable, heartfelt desserts. It’s also a business built to support the family and help sustain everyday living.

Why do you do it?

We do it to earn a living—honestly and proudly. Baking is our way of turning skill and effort into income while providing for our family.

History of the company

Creamy Treats began in London, where baking was done as a small business. Due to work commitments, operations eventually slowed down. When we arrived in the Philippines around 2009, baking became our primary source of income, focusing on cakes made for customers and the community.
